Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

1. Enterprise B2B Services Nature

NordicVista Ventures provides custom, enterprise-grade cloud architecture setups, app deployments, and DevOps integrations. Because of the highly customized nature of B2B infrastructure consulting services, the standard consumer protection withdrawal rights (e.g., the EU 14-day cooling-off period) do not apply.

2. Contract-Based Projects & Milestones

Refunds and cancellations are strictly governed by the Service Level Agreement (SLA) and the Master Services Agreement (MSA) signed prior to project initiation.

  • Milestone Payments: Infrastructure engineering projects are divided into structured milestones. Once a milestone is signed off and payment is cleared, that tranche is non-refundable, as the architecture and labor have been expended.
  • Imminent Tranches: Cancellation is not permitted for active, mid-development sprints. Notice of cancellation must be provided at least 14 days before the start of the next defined project milestone.

3. Cloud Infrastructure & DevOps Retainers

For ongoing continuous delivery (CI/CD) maintenance and architectural retainers:

  • Cancellations require a standard 30-day written notice to operations@nordicvista.eu to allow for secure off-boarding and complete handoff of all infrastructure states to your team.
  • Pre-paid infrastructure costs (AWS/Azure allocations) handled by NordicVista on behalf of the client are strictly non-refundable once provisioned.

4. Technical Dispute Resolution

If you believe a delivered architecture fails to meet the agreed-upon technical specifications, we will initiate a rigorous technical audit and remediation sprint at no extra cost, rather than issuing a direct refund. Client satisfaction and structural integrity remain our highest priority.
