Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 11, 2026

At NordicVista Ventures (accessible from https://www.nordicvista.eu), one of our main priorities is transparency. We operate under a strict zero-data footprint model regarding client application data. This Privacy Policy documents only the information collected by NordicVista Ventures regarding web visitors and business communications in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679.

1. Data Controller Details

NordicVista Ventures acts as the Data Controller solely for web visitor and client contact data. We do NOT act as a Data Processor for client environments. For inquiries regarding your privacy, contact us at legal@nordicvista.eu.

2. Information We Collect

We process personal data transparently and only for specified, explicit, and legitimate business communication purposes:

  • Corporate contact details (Name, Work Email, Company Name, Job Role) provided via our secure inquiry channels.
  • Technical configurations, server logs, and IP addresses exclusively linked to the use of our promotional website.
  • Billing information necessary for the execution of B2B infrastructure consulting.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (Article 6 GDPR)

We process your basic contact data strictly under the following lawful bases:

  • Contractual Necessity: To provide cloud app deployment and DevOps engineering consultancy.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our own website infrastructure and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: For voluntary business communications, which can be withdrawn at any time.

4. Data Subject Rights (GDPR Chapter III)

As an EU resident, you are guaranteed the following rights regarding the personal contact data we hold:

  • Right to Access (Art. 15): Request copies of your personal contact data.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate contact data.
  • Right to Erasure / 'Right to be Forgotten' (Art. 17): Request deletion of your B2B contact data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18): Request limits on how we use your contact data.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): Request transfer of your data to another organization.

To exercise these rights, please contact legal@nordicvista.eu.

5. Data Localization and Zero-Data Processing

Our cloud deployment services set up localized EU infrastructure for your business (e.g., AWS/Azure regions). We strictly do not transfer, host, or process your proprietary application data. All newly provisioned infrastructure is handed over directly to your internal teams. While our technical execution involves an elite offshore engineering team in India, they operate purely as architects and builders with no access to live production user data.

6. Log Files and Essential Cookies

NordicVista Ventures follows a standard procedure of using log files for our own website security. We employ minimal, essential cookies required for session integrity. Analytical trackers are disabled by default unless explicitly consented to.
