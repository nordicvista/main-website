Enterprise Privacy Policy

Effective Framework Date: Current Operating Year

1. Limited Data Controllership

NordicVista Ventures acts as a Data Controller exclusively for basic web visitor and business contact inquiries. We strictly do not collect, host, or process client user data. Contact: legal@nordicvistaventures.eu.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing of standard B2B contact data relies on Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (contract necessity) and Article 6(1)(f) (legitimate operational security interests).

3. Data Subject Privileges

We provide automated interfaces for citizens to exercise access rights, structural erasure (Right to be Forgotten), and direct data portability for their basic contact information.

Data Processing Addendum (DPA) Status

1. Non-Processor Declaration

NordicVista Ventures provides infrastructure setup, DevOps, and cloud engineering consultancy. Because we do not store, access, or process proprietary client application data, we do not operate as a Data Processor under Article 28 of the GDPR.

2. Complete Client Sovereignty

All finalized cloud deployments, Terraform states, and server environments are handed over entirely to the client's internal team. The client retains 100% Data Controllership and Processor responsibilities for their respective end-users.

3. Zero-Data Footprint Guarantee

Our operational model ensures no cross-border data transfers of client user information occur through our enterprise services. We build the architecture, you hold the data.

Cookie Governance Model

1. Strictly Essential Components

Cryptographic session identifiers and CSRF tokens utilized to prevent structural hijacking. These cannot be disabled as they govern baseline platform security.

2. Non-Essential Analytics

Tracking utilities are disabled by default. Telemetry data is only gathered upon explicit opt-in confirmation, utilizing anonymized IP masking prior to storage.
